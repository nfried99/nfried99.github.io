About Us

Imbibe

Imbibe Cajun Kitchen is a little piece of the Crescent City right here in Chapel Hill. We have Cajun food, live music, fine wine, and 16 taps with a rotating selection of great local beers and ciders. Our food is the real deal. We are proud to bring some great New Orleans classics and family recipes to North Carolina. With muffalettas on bread from Gambino’s Bakery; po boys served on Leidenheimer New Orleans French Bread; fried gulf oysters; blackened catfish and shrimp; our signature gumbo; Cindy’s Creole crab bisque; and our famous NOLA garlic butter shrimp: you will not leave hungry. Owner and chef Mandey Brown hails from the Big Easy and has been a bar owner and restaurateur in Chapel Hill for the past decade. Cajun cooking is in her blood. Her mother Cindy’s bisque and bitchy pickles are beloved by our regulars: as are the Humble Cakes baked up right here in our kitchen. Mandey’s great grandfather, Paul Blangé, was a chef at Brennan’s Restaurant in New Orleans. His culinary innovations include the delicious Banana’s Foster, Trout Blangé, and many others. Come in and join us sometime for dinner, jazz, and drinks. We’ll make you feel right at home.

Zog’s

Zog’s Pool Hall is our dog-friendly upstairs dive bar with all the cocktails, games, fun, friends, you could ask for. The eclectic decor and delicious signature Zogtails will have you feeling like you are hanging out right in your own living room (if your living room were cooler and had a bartender). Bring your friends and grab a surprise beer. Shoot a game of pool with a new friend. Bring your dog and chill out with a Zogtail. Drop some quarters into our vintage Skee-Ball machine or play a game of darts. Or you can just sit at the bar and read a book. It’s up to you. We throw parties for Mardi Gras, Halloween, our own birthday, and various other holidays. You’re guaranteed to have a good time. A truly unique Chapel Hill experience.
